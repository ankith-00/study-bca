MATRICES

A matrix is a rectangular array of numbers which act as information storage. OR Matrix is an arrangement of numbers in rows and columns and enclosed by brackets.

  • The numbers that form the matrix are called elements of the matrics.
  • The matrix A is noted As (aij) OR {aij} OR [aij] i.e A = (aij) i × j.
  • Matrics are usually denoted by capital letters (A - Z) and elements are represented as small letters (a - z).
  • To be more precise m are rows and n are columns than, the order of the matrix is m x n or (m,n).
  • Suppose there are 4 - rows and 3 - columns in the matrix then the order of the matrix is 4×3 or span,(4,3)
